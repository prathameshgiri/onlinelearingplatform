About Us

Our Story

Empowering Learners Since 2020

LearnHub was founded with a simple belief: that quality education should be available to anyone willing to learn. What started as a small collection of programming courses has grown into a comprehensive platform offering 200+ courses across technology, design, business, and more.

Our expert instructors come from leading companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and Meta. They bring real-world experience directly to your screen, ensuring every lesson is practical, relevant, and immediately applicable.

Today, LearnHub serves over 50,000 students across 120+ countries, with a 95% satisfaction rate and thousands of success stories from learners who've transformed their careers through our platform.
