Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about LearnHub

How do I create an account?

+

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner. Fill in your name, email, and create a password. You'll be able to start browsing and enrolling in courses immediately after registration.

How do I enroll in a course?

+

Browse our course catalog, click on a course you're interested in, and click the "Enroll Now" button on the course detail page. Make sure you're logged in to your account first.

Can I access courses on mobile devices?

+

Yes! LearnHub is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Learn anywhere, anytime at your convenience.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

+

Yes, upon completing 100% of the course content, you'll receive a digital certificate of completion. You can share this certificate on LinkedIn and other professional platforms.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Rupay), UPI, Net Banking, and popular wallets like Paytm and PhonePe. EMI options are also available on select courses.

Is there a refund policy?

+

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all courses. If you're not satisfied with a course, contact our support team within 7 days of enrollment for a full refund.

How do I track my progress?

+

Your progress is automatically tracked as you complete lessons. Visit your Dashboard to see progress bars for each enrolled course, average completion percentage, and completed courses.

How can I contact support?

+

You can reach us at support@learnhub.com or call +91 98765 43210 (Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 6 PM IST). You can also use the contact form on our Company page.

Your Privacy Matters

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, and course enrollment data. We also automatically collect usage data such as pages visited, time spent on courses, and device information to improve your learning experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and personalize our educational services, track your learning progress, send course recommendations, process payments, and communicate important updates. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption (SSL/TLS), secure data storage, access controls, and regular security audits. Your password is encrypted and we never store it in plain text.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and user preferences (like dark mode). Analytics cookies help us understand how users interact with our platform. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for payment processing, analytics, and email communications. These services have their own privacy policies and we ensure they meet our data protection standards.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You can also opt out of marketing communications. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@learnhub.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our platform. Continued use of LearnHub after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
